# Montana Tech

## Alignment with Montana Tech

The Butte Forge is aligned with Montana Tech's mission of producing
engineers and scientists who are prepared to solve real problems facing
industry and society. Projects are inspired by challenges relevant to
Montana and similar environments, including industrial systems, environmental
monitoring, remote sensing, and infrastructure operating under constrained
or off-grid conditions.

Participation in The Butte Forge enhances the core curriculum by providing
depth, integration, and professional context, particularly for students
approaching graduation who seek to strengthen their technical maturity,
leadership capacity, and readiness for advanced industry or research roles.

## Institutional Support

The Butte Forge is not operating in isolation.

The Chancellor of Montana Technological University is aware of this
initiative, has reviewed our vision, and has expressed clear support for
its mission.

What we are building here is aligned with the direction and purpose of
Montana Tech.
