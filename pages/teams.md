# The Four Pillars of The Forge

The Butte Forge is organized into four departments, each focused on a
critical domain of modern engineering. Members may contribute across
departments as projects demand.

---

## Department of Software Engineering

Responsible for application development, firmware, embedded software,
and the software tools that support every project in The Forge. This
department builds the code that brings hardware to life.

**Focus areas:** firmware development, application programming, version
control, testing, and software architecture.

---

## Department of Hardware Engineering

Covers digital and analog electronics, PCB design, sensor integration,
and physical prototyping. This department designs and builds the circuits
and boards that power our systems.

**Focus areas:** circuit design, PCB layout, soldering and assembly,
component selection, and hardware debugging.

---

## Department of Modeling & AI

Focuses on enabling large language models and other AI systems to interact
with the physical world. This department builds digital twins, agent
frameworks, and the data infrastructure that connects models to real
hardware and environments.

**Focus areas:** LLM tool use and agents, digital twins, simulation,
sensor data pipelines, model training and deployment, and edge inference.

---

## Department of Systems & Operations

Handles integration, deployment, networking, DevOps, and the operational
infrastructure that keeps projects running. This department ensures that
systems work reliably in the field.

**Focus areas:** Linux administration, networking, CI/CD, monitoring,
containerization, and field deployment.
